Baking That Means Something

Bradley Johnson has been baking his whole life. Through 20+ years of retail management, through career transitions, through everything — the kitchen has always been a constant. Baking is where precision meets creativity, where patience produces something you can share with people you care about.

When BITBShop came together as a business, it felt natural to include the bakery not just as a side offering, but as a genuine part of the identity. In a small town like Waynesville, relationships matter more than advertising. Showing up with a warm loaf or a birthday cake builds more trust than any business card.

Small Batches, On Purpose

We deliberately bake in small batches. This isn't a limitation — it's a philosophy. Small batches mean better control over quality, fresher ingredients, and a more personal product. Every cake, every loaf, every dozen cookies gets proper attention.

Pre-orders are encouraged because they let us plan, source the best ingredients, and dedicate the right time to your order.
