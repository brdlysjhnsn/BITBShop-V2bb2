Baking Has Always Been Part of the Story

Long before BITBShop was a business, the kitchen was where Bradley felt most at home. Baking through every job, every move, every life change — it's never not been part of the daily routine.

Now it's part of the business. Not because it had to be, but because it felt right. In a small town like Waynesville, showing up for people matters — and sometimes showing up means arriving with something warm from the oven.

Every baked good from BITBShop is made in small batches, by hand, with quality ingredients. No shortcuts. The same attention to detail Bradley brings to IT, he brings to the kitchen.
