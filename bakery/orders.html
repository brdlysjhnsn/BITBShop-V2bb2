How Custom Orders Work

Custom orders are how we do our best work. Whether it's a retirement cake, a box of themed cookies for a kid's birthday, or a dozen loaves of banana bread for a church event — the more details you share, the better the result.

  • 1

    Submit Your Request

    Fill out the form with your order details, event date, and any design ideas. Photos are welcome and helpful.

  • 2

    Bradley Gets Back to You

    Expect a response within 24 hours. Bradley will confirm availability, discuss details, and provide a quote.

  • 3

    Confirm & Pay Deposit

    A 50% deposit confirms your order. Balance is due at pickup. Payment by cash, Venmo, or CashApp.

  • 4

    Fresh Pickup in Waynesville

    Your order is ready on the agreed date — fresh-baked and ready to impress. Local delivery available for large orders.

Lead time: Most custom orders need at least 5–7 days notice. For wedding cakes or large event orders, please contact us 3–4 weeks ahead.
